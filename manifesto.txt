The Scavenger Grid is a revolutionary approach to local computing. Instead of relying on expensive cloud servers, it harvests the latent compute power of everyday office computers. 
When a legal aide steps away to get coffee, the grid awakens. It slices massive PDF case files into tiny semantic vectors, processing them locally.
The moment the user touches their mouse, the Ghost Exit protocol fires. Hardware interrupts instantly suspend the AI workload, ensuring zero performance degradation for the human user.
By utilizing SQLite-Vec and mDNS auto-discovery, the grid is entirely fault-tolerant and requires zero network configuration.
